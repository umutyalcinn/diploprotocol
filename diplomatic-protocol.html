The Language That Speaks Without Words

Diplomatic protocol is the codified set of rules and procedures governing behavior, communication, and ceremonial actions in interactions between states, embassies, international organizations, corporate executives, and their teams.

Who Should Attend

Our specialized training in Diplomatic Protocol is designed for a wide range of professionals who interact at high levels of diplomacy and business:

  • Employees of government institutions
  • Embassy and consular staff
  • PR and protocol departments
  • Assistants to high-ranking officials and delegations
  • Corporate executives, client-facing teams, and employees in all sectors
  • Staff in tourism, hospitality, hotels, and service industries who engage directly with VIP clients, partners, and international guests
  • Corporate communications and client relations staff representing their organizations in domestic and global contexts

This program equips professionals with the knowledge, cultural awareness, and refined behavior necessary to excel in formal, diplomatic, and high-profile corporate settings, ensuring that every interaction communicates respect, credibility, and authority.
