Operational & Corporate Protocol – Mastery Behind Every Seamless Event

Corporate protocol encompasses the rules, standards, and procedures that govern behavior, communication, and the organization of events within and between companies. Its purpose is to ensure professionalism, respect for hierarchy, and a strong corporate image.

These protocols are applied in corporations, banks, large enterprises, and B2B relationships, and include:

  • Respecting titles, positions, and hierarchy both internally and towards external partners
  • Organizing business meetings, conferences, ceremonial events, and contract signings
  • Establishing standards of professional conduct (e.g., introductions, business card exchanges, dress code, meeting etiquette)
  • Welcoming and hosting business partners and delegations
  • Communicating with media and the public in line with corporate values
  • Adhering to international and local norms in collaborations with foreign partners

Operational protocol focuses on the technical and logistical execution that ensures every event, visit, or ceremony is flawlessly organized.

It includes:

Planning and implementing events with a minute-by-minute schedule

Managing guests, VIPs, and delegations with precision

Coordinating arrivals, transfers, accommodation, and catering

Overseeing security, logistics, hostesses, drivers, and event spaces

Arranging seating, flags, speeches, gifts, and ceremonial details

Aligning all participants—from technical staff to protocol officers and organizers—for a perfectly synchronized experience

  • Event management and protocol teams
